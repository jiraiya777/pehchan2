Pathsala

Objective: To provide basic education, stationery to children

Program started in: Sept ‘17


It is important to continuously monitor every child individually, tutions are conducted every day at place near to their (children's) home. Groups are created among volunteers and each group is assigned one day in a week, in which thursday is a given holiday. The tutions are conducted for 1 hrs 30 min from 4:30 pm to 6:00 pm. Children are divided into two groups namely Shera and Tiger according to their education levels. Our future project, this year, under this program include training the children for JNV school entrance examination.

The activities done in school and the way to improve their performance is taken care off under this program. This program is helping children to be in continuous process of seeking which had impact on their performance in their school.
