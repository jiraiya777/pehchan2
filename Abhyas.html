Abhyas

Objective: To indulge children in various activities

Program started in: July 2018


Besides education there is necessity to indulge children in various activities. These aid to their mental strength and growth which helps them in personality development from the smaller age. Knowing the importance of this Pehchaan team decided to work on this. In this process of this program every saturday, named Abhyas Diwas,children are let free to perform other activities like sports, physical exercise singing, dancing, painting, etc under the guidance of the Pehchaan volunteers. These sessions are motivating them to participate in the programs conducted in their school.
