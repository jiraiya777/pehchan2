Membership:


There shall be the following categories of members:-


(a) Ordinary Member:

Every member (Accept all other categories of members. ) of the Society shall be ordinary member and each member will contribute a minimum of Rs. 500/- annually.


(b) Life Members:

Any other person otherwise eligible for membership in category (i) and (ii) who pays a minimum lump sum contribution of Rs. 10,000/- shall be enrolled as a life member of the Society;

(c) Donor Member:

company or a firm or any institution or organization or a person may be enr olled as a Donor Member on payment of such contribution as the Governing Body may decide;


(d) Patron

The Director of the Indian Institute of Technology, Rupnagar shall eb Ex-Officio Patron of the Society.
Provided that the Governing Body may from time to time modify the rules regarding membership and the amount of contribution.


Admission Fee and Subscription;


The admission fee and subscription shall be as under unless otherwise revised by the Governing Body of the Society.


(a) Admission fee :
(1) 200/- for UG-PG Student from IIT Ropar.
(2) 500 for all Other than (a1).
(b) Subscription Rs. 500/- per annum.
(c) Life Membership fee Rs.10,000/- at one time;
