Vision & Mission


The team at Pehchaan Ek Safar has been working towards their envisioned goals. We intend to educate the community around Ropar. This includes people from all ages, class and gender. Provide the community with proper education on health and environmental concerns and how to deal with them.


Provide them skills to sustain themselves and earn some livelihood. Inculcate rationality and scientific temper amongst the community.

We also intend to spread our wings around the country and work for bringing a positive change in our society.
