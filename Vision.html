Vision

Sustainability, social equality and the environment are now business problems. And corporate leaders can't depend on governments to solve them..


- Peter Senge, Founder, Society for Organisational Learning

Inspired by Senge's philosophy, a group of young corporate professionals
came together in 2002 to set up Smile Foundation to work with grassroots
initiatives for effecting positive changes in the lives of underprivileged
children, their families and communities.
